OGBOGU COTTAGE HOSPITAL

Ogbugu Cottage Hospital is a community-based healthcare facility established to provide accessible and affordable medical services to the people of Ogbugu and surrounding communities. The hospital is designed to deliver quality primary and secondary healthcare, including maternal and child care, emergency response, preventive medicine, and general outpatient services. With a focus on improving rural healthcare delivery, Ogbugu Cottage Hospital serves as a vital center for reducing health disparities, promoting wellness, and enhancing the overall quality of life for residents....

Status: Completed

ROAD CONSTRUCTION

Oboburu Road Construction is a vital infrastructure project aimed at enhancing connectivity and accessibility within Oboburu community and its environs. The project is designed to improve transportation, boost economic activities, and ease the movement of people, goods, and services. By providing a durable and modern road network, the construction will not only reduce travel time and costs but also contribute to the overall development and wellbeing of the community. ...

Status: Completed

EREMA COOKING GAS PLANT

Erema Cooking Gas Plant is an energy project established to provide safe, reliable, and affordable Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) to households and businesses within Erema community and surrounding areas. The plant is designed to promote cleaner energy usage, reduce dependence on firewood and kerosene, and contribute to environmental sustainability. By ensuring steady gas supply and creating employment opportunities, Erema Cooking Gas Plant plays a key role in improving living standards and fostering local economic growth...

Status: Completed
